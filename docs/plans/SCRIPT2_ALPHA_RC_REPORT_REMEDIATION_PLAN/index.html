    1. Objective

    Bring alpha_rc to the front of Script 2 generated reporting without deleting useful PnL analysis.

    The thesis-correct reading is:

    • alpha_rc answers whether the model or ensemble produced risk-constrained alpha relative to the same market period.
    • PnL answers whether the strategy made or lost absolute money.
    • Benchmark RC answers whether the market regime itself was favorable or unfavorable.
    • PnL remains necessary, but it must no longer be treated as the primary evidence of skill.

    The remediation goal is therefore not "replace PnL everywhere". The goal is:

    1. Every report section that ranks, concludes, filters, or interprets by PnL must explicitly justify that PnL lens.
    2. Every thesis-facing PnL lens must either be paired with alpha_rc or moved behind an alpha_rc primary lens.
    3. Correlation reports must keep PnL correlations but add parallel alpha_rc correlations.
    4. Extra-backtest reports must keep PnL deltas but add delta_alpha_rc and alpha-RC improvement summaries.
    5. Progress dashboards must keep objective/PnL status while surfacing alpha RC in the summary fields that currently influence interpretation.

    2. Metric Contract

    2.1 Primary thesis metric

    alpha_rc is the primary thesis metric for trading edge.

    Interpretation:

    • positive alpha_rc: the trial outperformed the risk-constrained benchmark for the same period;
    • negative alpha_rc: the trial underperformed that benchmark;
    • high PnL with low or negative alpha_rc: the market may have done most of the work;
    • negative PnL with positive alpha_rc: the trial can still be useful evidence in a losing market regime.

    2.2 Secondary economic metrics

    PnL, return, total gross PnL, net/trade, gross/trade, friction, Sharpe, win rate, and trade count remain valid. They answer different questions:

    Metric Keep? Primary use
    alpha_rc Yes Thesis skill / edge relative to the regime-matched benchmark.
    benchmark_return_risk_constrained Yes Market/regime context for the same fold/backtest window.
    stage3_total_pnl / total_pnl Yes Absolute economic outcome.
    stage3_return_pct / return_pct Yes Absolute normalized return.
    stage3_total_gross_pnl / total_gross_pnl Yes Gross signal before friction.
    total_friction Yes Execution-cost pressure.
    net_per_trade / gross_per_trade Yes Trade quality and friction sensitivity.
    stage3_trades_count / trades_count Yes Turnover/selectivity context.
    Sharpe / Win% Yes Distributional quality and risk diagnostics.
